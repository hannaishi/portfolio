Halepuna Waikiki

Halepuna Waikiki is a luxury boutique hotel located just a 2-minute walk from Waikiki Beach in the heart of Waikiki. It is surrounded by shops and dining options. This accommodation, designed by the world-renowned interior design firm "Champalimaud," partners with the Honolulu Museum of Art's public art program to showcase the works of several Hawaiian artists.

Information2

WiFi & Amenities

The rooms feature complimentary WiFi, a 50-inch flat-screen TV, a TOTO Washlet (electronic bidet), custom bath amenities, a deep soaking bathtub, and adjustable sunshades and lighting. They are also equipped with USB and Bluetooth connectivity.

Information3

Facilities

The hotel features an on-site fitness studio that hosts classes several times a week. Guests can also enjoy the SpaHalekulani, located across the street at the sister hotel. Additionally, there is a Halekulani Bakery & Restaurant on-site. At the bakery, you can even observe chocolate making. The restaurant, open all day, offers dishes made with locally sourced ingredients and provides both indoor and outdoor seating options.
